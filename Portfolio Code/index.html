MOBILE DESIGN

Common Garden App Design

The style of this app was inspired by vintage seed packets & 1950’s movie tickets. I captured this style with similar fonts, borders, and real vintage drawings. The booking process is streamlined to be clean, whimsical and enjoyable for the user. This app was created within the guidelines of Google Material 3 Design.

One Tap Checkout

This one tap checkout experience was designed to work with AR technology which scans a product using the device’s camera, then finds similar products online. The user experience is designed to use the least clicks possible to streamline the user through the purchase seamlessly.

Daily Planner App

This app is designed to be used as an everyday planner, with the added capability of being able to prioritize your to do list and schedule your day automatically. The screens are rich with information but still clean and organized for the user. The cute little drawings are there to add a small moment of joy to the user’s day.

Onboarding UX

These app onboarding screens are a simple, colorful, & inviting first experience with the app. As onboarding can be annoying for the user, there is an emphasis on wayfinding to show there are only three steps, adding interest with cute images, and making each step easily skippable.
