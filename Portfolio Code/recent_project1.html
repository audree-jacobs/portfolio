PROCESS

DRAFTING

The design for this site went through several versions until we narrowed in on the company’s vision. Drafts were animated in figma and viewable as interactive prototypes.

ASSET
CREATION

I created several digital assets for the website, using Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, & After Effects. These included logos, blueprints, back ground images, and 3D renderings.

PROTOTYPING

The final design was produced as a figma prototype to be presented to the CEO and approved for development.

STYLE
GUIDE

A style guide was created to be referenced by future editors in order to maintain branding consistency.
